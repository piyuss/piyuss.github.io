About me

Hello. I am a PhD candidate at North Carolina State University working towards a dual PhD in Engineering and Forestry. I work on the application of computer vision and robotics to biological systems.

I have worked on projects in high-throughput phenotyping of plants using digital images and in robotic systems for forestry applications. Some of the projects I have worked on are visualized in the slideshow below. Please use the links to publications and presentations on the navigation bar for a more complete view of my work.
